| **1. Design your process** | Choose how decisions will be made. Assemble a committee? Use scoring criteria? Each choice has trade-offs. |

| **4. Build your portfolio** | Evaluate 10-12 proposals with rich detail on milestones, teams, technology readiness, IP, and competitors. Decide what to fund. |
| **5. Manage over 8 quarters** | Review progress, respond to dilemmas, and decide: continue, accelerate, reduce, or kill each project. |
| **6. Add Year 2 projects** | New opportunities emerge. Add to your portfolio or stay focused? |
| **7. See results** | Discover what succeeded and failed. The board delivers their verdict. |
